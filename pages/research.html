Our Research

Current Projects & Initiatives

We conduct research to improve every stage of the evidence ecosystem, from evidence synthesis to guideline implementation.

Living Evidence Framework

Developing methodological frameworks and digital tools for living systematic reviews and living guidelines that keep clinical recommendations continuously updated with the latest evidence. This project explores optimal surveillance strategies, trigger mechanisms for updates, and efficient editorial workflows.

Methodology

Digital Evidence Ecosystem

Pioneering the vision of a fully Digital and Trustworthy Evidence Ecosystem where evidence flows seamlessly from research production through synthesis, guideline development, and point-of-care delivery. Working on interoperability standards and data exchange protocols.

Digital Innovation

Shared Decision Making

Researching innovative approaches to translate guideline recommendations into effective decision aids and patient-facing materials that support shared decision-making between clinicians and patients at the point of care.

Patient Engagement

AI-Enhanced Evidence Synthesis

Exploring the application of artificial intelligence and machine learning to improve the efficiency and accuracy of evidence synthesis, including automated screening, data extraction, and risk-of-bias assessment in systematic reviews.

Artificial Intelligence

GRADE Methodology Advancement

Contributing to the ongoing development and refinement of the GRADE (Grading of Recommendations Assessment, Development and Evaluation) framework, ensuring it remains the gold standard for evidence assessment in guideline development.

GRADE
