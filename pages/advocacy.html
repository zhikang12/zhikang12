Advocacy

Advocating for an efficient, digital, and trustworthy evidence ecosystem in global healthcare.

Advocating for an Efficient Evidence Ecosystem

We promote the implementation of a Digital and Trustworthy Evidence Ecosystem. Fuller adoption of this vision would minimize waste in healthcare delivery and evidence generation through enhanced collaboration and interoperability among ecosystem partners.

Connecting Stakeholders

Bringing together stakeholders across all stages of evidence creation and dissemination to foster collaboration, reduce duplication, and improve the flow of trustworthy evidence to decision-makers.

Living Evidence Alliance

Active participation in ALIVE (Alliance for Living Evidence), developing a coordinated marketplace for living evidence that ensures guidelines remain current and responsive to new research findings.

AI & Emerging Technologies

Demonstrating how the evidence ecosystem framework applies to emerging health technologies, particularly artificial intelligence, ensuring new tools are evaluated with the same rigor as traditional interventions.

Our Vision

A Digital & Trustworthy Evidence Ecosystem

Since articulating this vision in 2014, we have worked to build an interconnected system where evidence flows seamlessly from research production through synthesis, guideline development, and implementation at the point of care.

This ecosystem approach minimizes waste, reduces duplication of effort, and ensures that healthcare decisions worldwide are informed by the best available evidence. Our framework has become influential throughout the evidence-based medicine field.
