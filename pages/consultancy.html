Our Services

How We Can Help

We provide comprehensive consultancy services tailored to your organization's needs in evidence-based guideline development.

01

Digitalising Guideline Processes

Strategic guidance on transitioning your organization to digitally structured guidelines using the MAGICapp platform, improving efficiency and accessibility.

02

Living Guidelines Implementation

Expert consulting to help your organization evaluate and implement living guideline processes, ensuring your recommendations stay current with the latest evidence.

03

Methodological Support

Access to experienced guideline methodologists who provide tailored strategic advice, from GRADE methodology to systematic review integration.

04

Platform Migration

Full assistance migrating your existing guideline content into MAGICapp, with structured workflows for well-organized content transfer.

05

Training & Education

Virtual and in-person training sessions available globally in multiple languages, covering platform usage, methodology, and best practices.

06

Custom Development

Custom feature development and interactive summary-of-findings tools for specialized projects, including network meta-analyses visualizations.
