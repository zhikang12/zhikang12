The MAGICapp Platform

A digital guideline authoring and publishing platform built for the modern evidence ecosystem.

Platform Features

Everything You Need for Trustworthy Guidelines

MAGICapp provides a complete digital workflow for creating, managing, and disseminating evidence-based clinical guidelines.

Structured Authoring

Author guidelines in a structured, digital format using GRADE methodology. Create recommendations with linked evidence profiles and summary-of-findings tables.

Multi-language Publishing

Publish guidelines in multiple languages to reach global audiences. Support for translation workflows and version management across languages.

Living Guidelines

Support living guideline workflows with continuous evidence surveillance, rapid updates, and version tracking to keep recommendations current.

Decision Aids

Automatically generate interactive decision aids and patient-facing materials from your guideline recommendations to support shared decision-making.
