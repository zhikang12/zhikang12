About MAGIC

An independent, non-profit organization on a mission to improve patient care globally through trustworthy evidence-based guidance.

Our Mission

Transforming Healthcare Through Evidence

MAGIC Evidence Ecosystem Foundation is an independent, non-profit organization headquartered in Oslo, Norway. We are clinicians and evidence-based medicine experts dedicated to improving patient care globally.

Our mission is to enable the creation, dissemination, and implementation of trustworthy clinical guidance, bridging the gap between research evidence and clinical practice.

Our History

From Research Initiative to Global Impact

MAGIC emerged from a 2009 research initiative focused on addressing challenges in clinical practice guideline development. Our founders recognized that guidelines needed to be more trustworthy, more accessible, and more efficiently produced.

In 2013, we launched MAGICapp, our digital guideline authoring and publishing platform. By 2014, we had articulated our ambitious vision for a "Digital and Trustworthy Evidence Ecosystem" - a framework that has since become influential throughout the evidence-based medicine field.

Today, with over 5,400 public guidelines and partnerships with leading healthcare organizations worldwide, we continue to drive innovation in how evidence informs clinical practice.
