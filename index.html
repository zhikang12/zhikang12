Our Approach

How We Create Change

Through four interconnected pillars, we work to transform how clinical guidance is created, shared, and put into practice.

Research & Innovation

We undertake cutting-edge research and innovation projects to improve how clinical guidance is developed, adapted, disseminated, and implemented across healthcare systems worldwide.

MAGICapp Platform

We build our research innovations into the MAGICapp digital guideline platform, enabling organizations to author, publish, and update trustworthy guidelines efficiently.

Organization Support

We equip healthcare organizations with expert consultancy, methodological guidance, and training to create and maintain high-quality, trustworthy clinical guidelines.

Implementation Support

We create innovative dissemination and implementation products to support the uptake of guidance at the point of care, ensuring evidence reaches those who need it most.
